What You Get

Everything Your Gym Needs

From walk-in check-ins to automated renewals — every tool your team and your members expect, in one clean interface.

Member Management
Onboard new members in under 60 seconds. Track profiles, attendance history, and health goals from a single unified view.
Class Scheduling
Drag-and-drop timetable builder with capacity controls, waitlists, and automatic notifications when a spot opens up.
Billing & Payments
Recurring billing, UPI / card payments, invoice generation, and automatic expiry reminders — zero manual follow-ups.
Real-Time Analytics
Live dashboards for revenue, attendance trends, peak hours, and churn risk — so decisions are driven by data, not gut feel.
Automated Notifications
WhatsApp, SMS, and email alerts for upcoming renewals, class bookings, payment confirmations, and promotional campaigns.
Member App
Branded mobile app so members can book classes, track workouts, view their invoices, and connect with trainers — anytime.
Access Control
QR-code and biometric turnstile integration that validates membership status instantly at the entrance — no staff required.
Multi-Branch Support
Manage all your locations from one account. Members roam freely between branches; you see the full picture in one dashboard.
